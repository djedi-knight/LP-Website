- Biography -

CHESTER BENNINGTON

Date Of Birth : 3/20/76

INSTRUMENT: Vocals

GEAR:

  • Audio Technica Wireless Mics

Chester was born in Phoenix, Arizona. when he was 11 years old, his parents divorced. He has two half sisters and a half brother. Chester's powerful and emotional vocals is the focal point of the band for most fans. Whether it be expressing anger or rage through his harsh screams, or his feeling of dispairment and depression with his softler melodies, his lyrics will can always the souls of any listerner, who can instantly relate to them.

QUOTE:

"What I enjoy most about being in the band is having the opportunity to create and perform music with amazing musicians who have also become closest of friends. I would like to thank all who support us and make all of this possible." -Chester
