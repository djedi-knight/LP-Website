DAVID MICHAEL FARRELL

Date Of Birth : 2/8/77

INSTRUMENT: Bass

GEAR:

  • Ernie Ball Music Man Sting Ray basses
  • Ampeg SVT Classic heads
  • Ampeg SVT Classic cabinets
  • Dean Markley Blue Steel strings
  • Dunlop Picks (.88)
  • DBX 160 compressor
  • Monster cables
  • Sans Amp
  • Boss pedals
  • Whirlwind direct boxes
  • Shure wireless

David Michael (or "Phoenix") Farrell was born and raised in Plymouth, Massachusetts until later moving to Mission Viejo, CA, afterwhich he graduate from UCLA with a degree in philososophy. After plyaing with the original band Xero, Phoenix took a leave of absence from the band to tour with 'The Snax' for about a year. He then rejoined rejoined Linkin Park just in time to make it into the Crawling music video. Phoenix's catchy and memorable bass lines have the tendency to get stuck in your head. He gives anyone a good excuse to crank the bass when listening to it.

QUOTE:

"After a long year out of the band, it's been incredible to rejoin and be back in the mix. I appreciate everyone who supports us both on the website and at our live shows more than I could ever express. To be afforded the opportunity to do something I've always loved, with a bunch of friends, is truly a blessing. Thank You." -Phoenix
